Simple Cheese Toast

Recipe for Simple yet delicious Cheese Toast

Here's a super simple recipe for cheese toast with minimal ingredients and steps

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or any bread you prefer)
  • Butter or ghee
  • Grated cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or your favorite melting cheese)

Steps

  1. Preheat your Oven or Toaster Oven:

    Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F) or use a toaster oven.

  2. Prepare the Bread:

    Place the slices of bread on a baking sheet or toaster oven tray.

    Spread a thin layer of butter or ghee evenly on one side of each slice of bread. This will help the bread toast and add flavor.

  3. Add Cheese:

    Sprinkle a generous amount of grated cheese evenly over the buttered side of each slice of bread.

    You can use as much cheese as you like, depending on how cheesy you want your toast.

  4. Bake or Toast:

    Bake or toast for about 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the bread edges are golden and crispy.

    Keep an eye on it to avoid burning.

  5. Serve:

    Optionally, you can cut the toast into smaller pieces or triangles for serving
